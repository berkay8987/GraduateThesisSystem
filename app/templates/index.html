This is the Graduate Thesis System

The Graduate Thesis System (GTS) is a comprehensive platform designed to help users explore, manage, and edit data related to theses, researchers, universities, and institutes. With its intuitive and user-friendly interface, GTS provides an efficient way to search, organize, and update academic resources, fostering collaboration and innovation in the research community. Whether you're looking to browse research topics, connect with experts, or maintain up-to-date records, GTS makes it simple and accessible.

View - GTS

Explore the Graduate Thesis System (GTS) and view available theses, researchers, universities, and institutes through an intuitive, user-friendly interface.

Edit - GTS

Make changes to existing theses, researchers, universities, and institutes in the Graduate Thesis System (GTS), with easy-to-use editing tools for data management.

Delete - GTS

Remove outdated or unnecessary theses, researchers, universities, and institutes from the Graduate Thesis System (GTS) with a streamlined deletion process.
